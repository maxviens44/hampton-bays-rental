House Info & Rules

Everything You
Need to Know

📶
Wi-Fi
  • Network: staythehamptons
  • Password provided after booking
  • Full coverage throughout house, office, and pool patio
🔑
Check In & Out
  • Check in after 4:00 PM
  • Check out by 11:00 AM
  • Smart lock code sent morning of arrival
  • Before leaving: run the dishwasher and take trash to outdoor bins
🗑️
Garbage & Recycling
  • Bins on the side of the house near the driveway
  • Trash pickup: Tuesday only
  • Recycling: Wednesday
  • Break down boxes and rinse cans and bottles
💡
Lighting & HVAC
  • Most lights are on dimmers — slide to adjust brightness
  • Pool and patio lights: switches near the back sliders
  • Thermostats: keep between 68–74°F for comfort and efficiency
🏊
Pool & Outdoor
  • Saltwater pool with weekly service — serviced every Thursday
  • 6 beach chairs and 1 umbrella are available in the garage, on the stairs leading up to the gym
  • Outdoor shower by the garage walkway
  • Close umbrellas and secure cushions if windy
  • Only plastic glasses, cups, and plates permitted at the pool and patio
🚗
Parking
  • Driveway fits 5 cars
  • Garage access only if noted in your booking
  • Avoid street parking — local signs apply
💻
Home Office
  • Dedicated desk in a quiet room with natural light
  • Fast Wi-Fi with strong signal in office and common areas
  • Standard outlets and a surge-protected power strip
🏋️
Gym
  • Accessible via the side door of the garage, up the stairs
  • Equipment: exercise bike, multi-station gym, dumbbells, and weight bench
  • Towels and a water cooler are provided
🚨
Emergency
  • Dial 911 for emergencies
  • Urgent home issues: use host phone number in your arrival email
  • Nearest hospital: Stony Brook Southampton Hospital (~20 min west)
📋
House Rules
  • No parties and no smoking
  • No pets unless approved before booking
  • Quiet hours after 10:00 PM per town guidance
  • Only plastic glasses, cups, plates, and dishes permitted at pool and patio
