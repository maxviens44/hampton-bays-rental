Welcome to our luxury Hamptons vacation rental in Hampton Bays, New York. This classic cedar shingle estate offers 3,750 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space, four bedrooms, five bathrooms, bay views, and a heated saltwater pool — everything you need for a perfect Hamptons stay.

The vaulted great room opens directly onto the pool patio, making summer entertaining effortless. The chef's kitchen features Viking appliances and Quartzite counters, while the primary suite occupies the entire north wing with a spa bath, jacuzzi, and bay-view balcony.

Located minutes from Shinnecock Hills Golf Club, Southampton Village, and the finest Hamptons beaches, this home is ideal for families, groups, summer rentals, golf trips, and extended getaways. Whether you're here for a long weekend or a full summer season, it's a rare combination of privacy, comfort, and location.
